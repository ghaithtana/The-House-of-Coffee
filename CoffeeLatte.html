CaffeeLatte recipe

Info

Time
15 mins
Difficulty
Servings
2

Ingredients

6
tablespoons coffee/espresso.
8
ounces water.
13
ounces milk.
1
tablespoons brown sugar.
1
Ground cinnamon

Directions

  1. Gather the ingredients..
  2. Pull your espresso shot in your latte cup and add a shot of flavored syrup for a flavored latte, if using...
  3. Using a spoon to retain the microbubbles on top of the steamed milk, pour the bottom 2/3 of the steamed milk from the steaming pitcher into the latte cup.
  4. Top the latte with the remaining bubbles. (You can spoon them onto your drink if desired.).
  5. If you'd like, feel free to add some latte art. Serve and enjoy!.
<

History:

As early as 1847 the English term Caffe Latto was used and then the essay Italian Journeys by William Dean Howells in 1867 who used the term caffe latte. Respected coffee author and reviewer Kenneth Davids has written that the Caffe Latte is basically an American invention and even in Italy it was first found in Italy only in places with predominantly American tourists. Some say the Caffe Latte was developed for Americans because the traditional Cappuccino was too strong so more steamed milk was added.
